Phone Holder v2.0

July 2026

In the making, below is my initial plan for v2.0

  • Use springs for an actual adjustable holder design
  • Create 2 battery system (maybe) with 1) a power bank with 5V/5A output to power the raspberry pi and 2) a 9V battery to power arduino (with arduino output powering the rest)
  • Design housing for portability and accessibility, with: 1) a volume less than 5” x 5” x 8”; 2) rounded edges; 3) a well-ventilated space for raspberry pi
  • Add a start button for autonomous operations
  • Add a breadboard to connect the start button, battery, servo motor, and Arduino
