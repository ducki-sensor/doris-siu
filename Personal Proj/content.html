Video Journal: From Kiama to Gerringong

2026

Music: “Rainy Day” by Sungha Jung

During my time studying abroad in Australia, the coastal trek from Kiama to Gerringong stood out as a highlight. The seamless blend of rolling green hills, rugged cliffs, and the Pacific coastline felt like a perfect landscape. To capture the scale of the scenery, I documented the journey through film and refined the footage using Videoleap. To maintain a cohesive narrative theme, I performed individual color correction and warmth adjustments on every clip. By manually grading the footage, I ensured a consistent visual atmosphere that captures the natural vibrance of the Australian coast.
