Asian Culture Club Snack Sale Poster

Mar 2023

As Treasurer of the Niskayuna Hgh School Asian Culture Club, I managed the financial logistics and creative promotion for a snack sale fundraiser. I digitally illustrated the promotional posters to drive student engagement, resulting in a record-breaking turnout that generated over $1,000 in gross sales and over $500 in net profit—a performance that significantly exceeded the club's typical fundraising benchmarks and provided a strong financial foundation for the year’s remaining cultural events.

Palm Beach

Jan 2026

What occupied my mind most while studying abroad in Sydney, Australia, were the countless surrounding beaches with breathtaking ocean views and extraordinarily clear water. Naturally, on a girls’ outing where we planned to paint by the shore, I chose to capture the ocean. Since there were several palm trees at the scene, I, of course, had to include one in my gouache painting.
