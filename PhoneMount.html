Motion-Tracking Phone Holder

SolidWorks Python (OpenCV) Arduino 3D Printing Laser Cutting Raspberry Pi Servo Motor Rapid Prototyping

As a competitive ballroom dancer, I often move out of frame when filming myself practice because of the dynamic movements. This inspired me to make a motion-tracking phone holder that follows a moving subject, allowing dancers to travel freely across the floor while having all details captured.

I am currently working on a second version based on what I have learned and wish to improve from making my first version.
